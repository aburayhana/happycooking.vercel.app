##### The ingredients needed to make Vegetable Curry:

1. Take 1/4 onion
1. Take 3 garlic cloves
1. Make ready 1 tbsp ginger
1. Get 1/2 cup vegetable or chicken broth
1. Take 1/2 cup coconut milk
1. Take 1 small shredded carrot
1. Make ready 1/2 red pepper, julienne cut
1. Make ready 1 handful green beans
1. Prepare 1/2 potato, peeled and cut into small chunks
1. Take 1 tsp ground cumin
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp turmeric
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp curry powder
1. Make ready 2 tbsp chopped cilantro
1. Get 1/2 cup peanuts




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Vegetable Curry:

1. Cut and julienne all vegetables. Put ginger and garlic cloves into a processor and finely chop.
1. Heat olive oil and a little bit of butter and cook onions until translucent, about 5 minutes.
1. Add in ginger and garlic mixture and cook another minute.
1. Add in chicken broth and coconut milk. Cover and simmer about 3 minutes. Add in all the spices (cumin, cayenne, curry, turmeric, salt and pepper) and let simmer all together for another few minutes.
1. Add in potatoes, cover, and cook a few more minutes until you can pierce the pieces with a fork.
1. Add in the rest of the vegetables and cook until done. Then add in cilantro and peanuts and cook an additional 2 minutes.
1. Add mixture on basmati or white rice. Enjoy!
